Ensure the sitemap updates automatically when new pages are added to the website.

STEP 3 — CREATE ROBOTS.TXT

Add or update the robots.txt file.

Example:

User-agent: *
Allow: /

Sitemap: https://tools.pixocraft.in/sitemap.xml

---

STEP 4 — ADD INDEXING SIGNALS

Ensure every page includes:

• canonical tag
• meta title
• meta description
• structured data (FAQ and HowTo schema)

STEP 5 — GOOGLE INDEXING READY

Ensure pages are optimized for search engines by:

• allowing crawling

• ensuring structured data is valid

STEP 6 — VALIDATION
