5. HOW TO ADD SIGNATURE IN GMAIL (DESKTOP)

VERY IMPORTANT

Steps:

1. Open Gmail
2. Click Settings (⚙️)
3. Click “See all settings”
4. Scroll to Signature section
5. Click “Create new”
6. Add text or upload image
7. Save changes
