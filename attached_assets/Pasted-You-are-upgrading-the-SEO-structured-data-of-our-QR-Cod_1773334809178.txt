STEP 1 — ADD FAQ SCHEMA

Every SEO page already contains an FAQ section.

   {
     "@type": "Question",

       "@type": "Answer",
       "text": "Enter your data in the QR generator tool, customize the design, and download the QR code."

     "name": "Are QR codes free to generate?",
     "acceptedAnswer": {

     }
   }
 ]
}
</script>

Generate this schema dynamically based on each page’s FAQ content.
