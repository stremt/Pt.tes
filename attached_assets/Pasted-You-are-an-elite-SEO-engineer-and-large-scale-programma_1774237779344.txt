* Wasted crawl budget

### 2. Category Pages Wrong Priority

Current:
/tools/text
/tools/image
/tools/pdf
/tools/media

Fix:

* Move ALL category pages to priority 0.8–0.85

Reason:

* These are structural authority pages
* They distribute ranking power across tools

### 3. Over-Inflated Tier 2 (MAJOR ISSUE)

Current:

* 150+ tools at 0.8

Problem:

* Google sees everything as equally important
* No page stands out → ranking dilution

Fix:

* Limit Tier 2 (0.8) to ONLY top 50–70 tools
* Move remaining tools to 0.6–0.7

### 4. Long-Tail Pages Too High

Examples:
/12-character-password-generator → 0.7
/strong-password-for-gmail → 0.7

Fix:

* Move ALL long-tail & variations to 0.5–0.6

Reason:

* These are support pages, not core authority pages
* Prevent crawl budget waste

### 5. Blog Priority Incorrect

Current:
/blogs → 0.8 ❌

Fix:

* Set blog index to 0.6–0.7 (until authority grows)
