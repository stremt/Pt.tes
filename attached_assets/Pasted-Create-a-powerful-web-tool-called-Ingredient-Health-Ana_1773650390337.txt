Create a powerful web tool called **“Ingredient Health Analyzer”** that helps users check whether a food product is healthy or unhealthy based on its ingredient list.

The tool should work directly in the browser and feel fast, simple, and intelligent.

The user will paste or type the ingredient list of any food product exactly as written on the product label. The tool should analyze the ingredients and generate a detailed health report.

The system must first **detect and extract individual ingredients** from the text input. Ingredients may be separated by commas, brackets, percentages, or mixed formatting, so the parser should intelligently normalize and clean the text before analysis.

After detecting the ingredients, the tool must analyze them individually and also evaluate their **combined health impact**.

For each ingredient, the system should identify:
• Ingredient name
• Ingredient category (sweetener, oil, preservative, stabilizer, additive, whole food, etc.)
• Health impact level (healthy, moderate, harmful, unknown)
• Short explanation of why it is considered healthy or risky

The tool must also detect **high-risk ingredient patterns**, such as:
• High sugar combinations
• Ultra-processed food indicators
• Artificial additives
• Excessive preservatives
• High glycemic ingredients
• Hydrogenated oils or unhealthy fats

The analyzer should then calculate an **overall product health score from 0 to 100**.

Score ranges should represent:

• 80–100 → Excellent / Healthy
• 60–79 → Good / Mostly Healthy
• 40–59 → Moderate / Processed
• 20–39 → Unhealthy
• 0–19 → Highly Unhealthy / Avoid

The report should include the following sections:

1. **Detected Ingredients List**
   Each ingredient should be shown with a small explanation and risk level.

2. **Ingredient Warnings**
   Show clear warnings for things like:
   • High sugar content
   • Artificial colors
   • Artificial flavors
   • Chemical preservatives
   • Ultra-processed ingredients

3. **Health Score**
   Display a large score indicator showing the overall product rating.

4. **Diet Suitability Analysis**
   The tool should identify which people should avoid the product, such as:
   • Diabetics
   • People on weight-loss diets
   • Children
   • People with high blood pressure
   • People avoiding processed foods

5. **Positive Ingredients**
   Highlight any beneficial ingredients like whole grains, nuts, oats, fiber sources, or natural ingredients.

6. **Final Verdict**
   Provide a short summary explaining whether the product is healthy, moderately safe, or unhealthy.

The tool should present the result in a clear and visually structured way so users can understand the health impact instantly.

The user experience should be extremely simple:
• Input ingredient list
• Click analyze
• Instantly receive a detailed health report

Make the tool feel like a **smart food label interpreter** that helps people quickly understand what they are actually eating.
